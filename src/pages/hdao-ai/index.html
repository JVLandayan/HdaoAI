About humanDAO: Improving Lives Through Crypto
humanDAO, founded in 2021, is a community-funded, social impact DAO. Our mission to create innovative solutions and forge meaningful partnerships has led us to collaborate with industry-leading NFT projects like Moonbirds, web3 projects such as Bankless DAO, and immersive web3 games like Thetan Arena. We are driven by a passion for empowering underserved communities, leveraging cutting-edge technology, and making a lasting impact on the world
