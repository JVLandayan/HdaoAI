Frequently Asked Question
What is the Human Task Force (HTF)?
The Human Task Force is an on-chain gig economy platform designed to connect users with skilled assistants from underserved communities countries. These assistants provide a wide range of services, including administrative tasks, graphic design, research, and lead generation, while earning web3 wages. The HTF also powers the data collection for humanDAO AI bots.
How do I purchase Energy for the HTF?
You can purchase Energy on-chain through our platform. This Energy acts as a currency that you can exchange for the services of assistants within the Human Task Force.
What types of tasks can HTF assistants perform?
HTF assistants can perform various tasks such as administrative work, graphic design, research tasks in web3 or web2, lead generation, and more. They are skilled and adaptable to meet your unique project requirements
How does the HTF benefit both users and assistants?
Users gain access to skilled assistance that helps them save time and streamline their work, while assistants from underserved communities receive valuable employment opportunities and fair wages, fostering a sustainable and inclusive ecosystem for growth
How does the HTF contribute to humanDAO AI bots?
The Human Task Force powers the data collection for humanDAO AI bots, ensuring accurate, up-to-date, and comprehensive information for the AI to provide bespoke insights tailored to each project's unique needs.
Can I request custom services or tasks from the HTF?
Yes, the HTF is designed to be flexible and adaptable to your specific needs. You can request custom services or tasks to suit your project requirements.
Was the Human Task Force previously known as Pocket Assistants?
Yes, the Human Task Force was formerly called Pocket Assistants. We decided to rebrand and improve the service to better highlight the powerful collaboration between our human workforce and AI products. The new brand name reflects the significant role of humans in driving the success of our platform and the AI-driven solutions we provide.
About humanDAO: Improving Lives Through Crypto
humanDAO, founded in 2021, is a community-funded, social impact DAO. Our mission to create innovative solutions and forge meaningful partnerships has led us to collaborate with industry-leading NFT projects like Moonbirds, web3 projects such as Bankless DAO, and immersive web3 games like Thetan Arena. We are driven by a passion for empowering underserved communities, leveraging cutting-edge technology, and making a lasting impact on the world
